Inspiration


Edward Lewis Martin

Born: 1935

Died: 2017

Edward Lewis Martin is my father and source for the artistic inspiration. Ed Martin was a Korean War veteran, loving husband to Sammie Martin and father of 2 sons. He wrote a book and was published as "Randy's World: The marauders". Please don't ask to buy his paintings from our family. They are not for sale.

This web site is deicated to the synthesis of science and art, the synergy of coding with creativity.
